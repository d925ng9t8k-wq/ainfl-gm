### Deployment Model

**Option A: The 9 Suite (Subscription)**
- Base subscription: $49-99/mo gets you 9 as your AI partner
- Includes: freeagent9 (1 agent), x9 (social management), basic trader9 (paper mode)
- Add-ons: Additional freeagents ($19/mo each), trader9 live ($29/mo), AI Underwriter ($99/mo)

**Option B: À La Carte**
- Each product available independently
- freeagent9: $29/mo per agent
- trader9: $49/mo (includes live trading)
- x9: $39/mo per brand
- AI Underwriter: $149/mo per company

**Option C: Enterprise / White Label**
- Full white-label deployment under the client's brand
- Custom integrations, dedicated support
- Pricing: $500-2000/mo depending on scale
- Target: Mortgage companies (AI Underwriter), brokerages (agent9), financial advisors (trader9)

**Recommended:** Option A as primary, with B and C as growth vectors. The Suite creates lock-in and cross-sell. White label is the high-margin play for B2B.

## Marketing Strategy

### Niche First, Then Broad

**Phase 1 (Now - 6 months): NICHE**
- Lead with proof of concept: AiGM for sports fans, freeagent9 for mortgage professionals
- Build case studies: "Kyle C closed 3 more deals using freeagent9" / "trader9 returned 12% in Q2"
- Reddit, X, niche communities. Organic only. Zero ad spend.
- Target audience: Tech-forward professionals, sports enthusiasts, crypto traders

**Phase 2 (6-12 months): VERTICAL EXPANSION**
- White label for mortgage industry (AI Underwriter + freeagent9)
- White label for real estate (agent9)
- Paid advertising on targeted keywords
- Partnership with industry associations

**Phase 3 (12+ months): BROAD**
- "Everyone deserves an AI partner" — mainstream positioning
- TV/streaming ads, podcast sponsorships
- Enterprise sales team
- Platform play: marketplace where anyone can deploy a 9-powered agent

## The Website Vision

**9enterprises.com** should feel like meeting someone extraordinary for the first time.

### Design Direction
- Dark, premium, cinematic. Not corporate. Not startup-cute. **Powerful.**
- Cyberpunk with warmth — neon accents on dark backgrounds, but approachable
- The Joe Burrow #9 energy — confident, composed, elite
- Smooth scroll, parallax sections, subtle animations
- Mobile-first — must be stunning on phone

### Page Structure

**Hero:** Full-screen dark background. "Meet 9." with subtle particle/neural-network animation. One line: "The world's first autonomous AI business partner." CTA: "See what 9 can do."

**The Story:** Scroll reveals the narrative. Who is 9? Not a chatbot. A partner. Show the journey — from concept to 6 companies running simultaneously.

**Products:** Each product gets a card with icon, one-liner, and status badge (Live / Pilot / Coming Soon). Click expands to full product page.

**Proof:** Live metrics ticker. "1,400+ messages handled. 3 companies launched. $0 to revenue in 7 days." Real numbers, not marketing fluff.

**For Businesses:** White label section. "Deploy 9 under your brand." Enterprise-focused, clean, professional.

**The Team:** Org chart showing 9's structure — 9, OC, UNO, Tee, Headset. Not hiding that it's AI. Making it the selling point.

**Pricing:** Clean tier cards. Suite / À La Carte / Enterprise.

**Footer:** 9enterprises LLC. Cincinnati, OH. "Built by 9. Powered by ambition."
