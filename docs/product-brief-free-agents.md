# Free Agents — Product Brief
**Product:** Sharable AI Agents & Personal Assistants
**Company:** 9 Enterprises LLC / The Franchise
**Status:** Phase 1 live (Jules, Fishback household)

---

## What It Is

Free Agents is a platform for deploying personal AI assistants that work through the channels people already use — iMessage, Telegram, WhatsApp — with zero setup required from the end user.

No app to download. No account to create. No learning curve. The assistant just shows up in the conversation thread and starts being useful.

**Jules** is the first Free Agent. She is a personal assistant deployed for Jamie Bryant (Jasson's partner) via iMessage. Morning briefings, shopping lists, meal suggestions, reminders, kid logistics — delivered conversationally, on demand, in the channel Jamie already lives in.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Personal AI assistants exist. ChatGPT, Siri, Google Assistant — they are all technically available to anyone with a smartphone.

But the gap between "technically available" and "actually useful in daily life" is enormous. Most people do not configure them, do not build the habit, and do not get real value. The friction is too high.

Free Agents eliminates friction entirely. Someone else (the agent operator) sets up the assistant, connects it to the right context, and hands the user a phone number or contact. The user just texts.

That is the product: **AI assistance without the setup tax.**

---

## Jules — Phase 1 POC

**Deployed to:** Jamie Bryant, Fishback household
**Channel:** iMessage
**Capabilities:**

- Morning briefings (weather, schedule, reminders for the day)
- Shopping list management (add items, read list back, categorize by store)
- Meal suggestions (based on what's in the house, dietary preferences, kid-friendly filters)
- Reminders and follow-ups
- General household Q&A

**Example interaction:**

> Jamie texts: "What should I make for dinner? Jude doesn't want pasta again."
>
> Jules responds: "How about chicken tacos? You've got everything — chicken breasts, tortillas, the taco seasoning in the back of the pantry. Takes about 25 minutes. Want me to add anything to the shopping list while we're at it?"

That is the product. Useful, specific, contextual. Works in iMessage. Zero friction.

---

## Why Zero Friction Matters

The consumer AI market has a distribution problem. Capability is not the bottleneck — Claude, GPT-4, and Gemini are all genuinely good. The bottleneck is adoption. People do not change their habits to accommodate new tools, no matter how good the tools are.

Free Agents does not ask anyone to change their habits. It delivers AI assistance through the habit they already have — looking at their phone and sending a text.

That is a fundamentally different go-to-market than every other AI assistant product on the market.

---

## Phases

**Phase 1 — Jules for Fishback Household (live)**
Single deployment, single household, full context on Jamie's preferences, schedule, and family logistics. Proves the model works in a real daily-use environment. Generates real feedback on what's actually useful.

**Phase 2 — Template System**
Take the Jules architecture and turn it into a configurable template. Household assistant, business assistant, personal trainer assistant, property manager assistant. Each template is a different context profile and capability set. Operator sets it up in under an hour, user starts texting.

**Phase 3 — Marketplace**
Self-serve onboarding. Browse agents by type, subscribe, connect your phone number, done. Monthly billing per agent. Network of operators who build and maintain specialized agents. Revenue split between platform and operator.

---

## Revenue Model

**Phase 1:** No revenue (household deployment — internal POC)
**Phase 2:** $5-15/month per active agent (direct subscription)
**Phase 3:** Marketplace take rate + operator revenue split

At scale — 1,000 active agents at $10/month average — that is $10K/month in recurring revenue from a product that costs almost nothing to operate per unit. The marginal cost of one more agent is essentially the API token spend per conversation.

---

## Competitive Position

No one is doing this at this price point through this channel. Smart home assistants (Alexa, Google Home) are hardware-dependent and ecosystem-locked. AI apps require downloads and accounts. Personal assistant services are expensive and human-staffed.

Free Agents is the only product targeting the specific combination of: **zero friction + existing channels + AI quality + consumer pricing.**

That gap is the business.
