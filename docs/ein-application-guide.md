## Why You Need an EIN

An EIN (Employer Identification Number) is the LLC's federal tax ID. You need it to open a business bank account, file taxes, and hire contractors. It is free and takes about 5 minutes online.

## How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

The IRS online EIN application is at:
**https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online**

This is a live, interactive web application -- it cannot be automated or submitted via API. Jasson must complete it in a browser. The application is available Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm Eastern.

### Step 1: Click "Apply Online Now"

Go to the IRS URL above. Click the **"Apply Online Now"** button. Then click **"Begin Application"** on the next page.

### Step 2: Select Entity Type

- Select **"Limited Liability Company (LLC)"**
- Click **Continue**

### Step 3: LLC Details

- **Number of members:** Select **1** (single-member LLC)
- **State where LLC was organized:** Select **Ohio**
- Click **Continue**

### Step 4: Why Are You Applying?

- Select **"Started a new business"**
- Click **Continue**

### Step 5: Responsible Party Information

This is where Jasson's personal info goes:

- **First Name:** Jasson
- **Middle Name:** Alan
- **Last Name:** Fishback
- **SSN:** Enter your SSN (this is the step that requires manual entry)
- Check the box: "I am a responsible party for this LLC"
- Click **Continue**

### Step 6: Business Address

### Step 7: Business Details

### Step 8: Type of Business

- Select the category that best describes 9 Enterprises LLC
- For a tech/consulting/holding company, select **"Other"** and type a brief description (e.g., "Technology consulting and software development")

### Step 9: Tax Classification

- Since this is a single-member LLC, the IRS defaults to **Disregarded Entity** (taxed as sole proprietor)
- If asked on Line 9a, check **"Other"** and write **"disregarded entity"**
- This means LLC income goes on your personal 1040 Schedule C
- Click **Continue**

### Step 10: Confirm and Submit

- Review all information
- Click **Submit**
- You will receive your EIN immediately on screen
- **Save/print the confirmation letter (CP 575)** -- this is your official EIN assignment notice

### Step 11: After You Get the EIN

- Screenshot or print the confirmation page
- The IRS will also mail a CP 575 letter to 655 Nordyke Rd within 4 weeks
- Send the EIN number to 9 via Telegram so it can be added to the vault

## Important Notes

- The application is **free** -- do not pay any third-party site
- The online tool only works during IRS hours: **Mon-Fri, 7am-10pm ET**
- Only one EIN can be issued per responsible party per day
- You need the SSN at the keyboard -- there is no workaround
- If the online tool is down, the backup method is IRS Form SS-4 by fax (fax to 855-641-6935, EIN returned within 4 business days)
