## Product-by-Product Viability

### AI Underwriting Team — HIGHEST COMMERCIAL VIABILITY
- **TAM:** ~5,000 independent mortgage companies, 5-100 LOs each
- **Pricing:** $500-2,000/month per lender
- **ARR ceiling:** $37.5M at 1% market penetration
- **Competitive advantage:** Voice-capable (unique), lender-specific overlays, internal deployment
- **Comparable:** Guideline Buddy exists at $12/month — but it's text-only, no overlays, no voice
- **Enterprise comparables:** Tavant, ICE Encompass AI ($200K+ implementations)
- **Verdict:** This is the clearest path to real B2B revenue. Rapid Mortgage as anchor customer = instant case study.

### Free Agents / Jules — HIGH VIABILITY, NEEDS SCALE
- **TAM:** Potentially massive (300M+ Americans don't use AI assistants)
- **Pricing:** $5-15/month per agent
- **Competitive advantage:** Zero-friction, gifting model, existing channel delivery
- **Comparable:** Poke ($15M raised, $29-292/month, tech-savvy only)
- **Verdict:** Real product, real gap. But consumer plays are slower to monetize. The template system (Phase 2) is where it becomes scalable.

### AiNFLGM.com — MODERATE VIABILITY, NEEDS TRAFFIC
- **TAM:** $37.4B global fantasy sports market, NFL = 74.9%
- **Revenue model:** Affiliate ($100-400 CPA), AdSense, premium tier
- **Competitive advantage:** No direct AI-powered GM simulator exists at this depth
- **Comparable:** StickToTheModel (free, 500K+ sims, desktop-focused)
- **Verdict:** Product is strong, traffic is the bottleneck. Not a traditional SaaS sale — it's a content/advertising business.

### 9 Operating Model — HIGHEST STRATEGIC VALUE
- **What it is:** The playbook for running a multi-agent AI business
- **Who buys it:** Other entrepreneurs who want to do what Jasson is doing
- **Format:** Consulting, licensing, or a course/community
- **Verdict:** This is the meta-product. If The Franchise proves the model works, the model itself becomes the most valuable asset. "How one founder with AI built and runs 4 products simultaneously."
