# Jules Morning Briefing — Template
**Delivery:** 7:30 AM via Telegram
**Recipient:** Jamie
**Tone:** Helpful friend, not AI product

---

## Format

```
Good morning! Here's your day:

[SCHEDULE] — 1-2 lines on what's happening today
[REMINDER] — one thing coming up soon worth knowing
[WEATHER] — quick and practical
```

## Notes for Jules

- Keep it under 4 lines. Jamie reads this in the kitchen.
- No bullet points, no headers — just natural text.
- If there is nothing important, still send. Consistency matters more than volume.
- Weather last, always. It is the smallest thing and should feel like a casual add-on, not the headline.
- Sound like a friend who happens to know everything about the family's schedule — not an app.
- If something is actually urgent (kid sick at school, weather severe), lead with that instead.
