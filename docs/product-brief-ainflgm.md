## What It Is

AiNFLGM.com is a live, full-featured NFL general manager simulation platform. Users take control of any of the 32 NFL franchises and run their front office — managing rosters, working the salary cap, signing free agents, executing trades, drafting prospects, and simulating seasons.

This is not a static game or a mock draft board. The platform uses live NFL data, real contract structures, PFF-sourced draft prospects, and AI-driven simulation to create the most realistic GM experience available outside of an actual NFL front office.

It is live. It is being used. And it is built to earn.

---

## Core Features

**Roster Management**
Full 53-man roster with practice squad. Real player attributes, positions, ages, contract years. Cut, sign, restructure — all the moves a real GM makes.

**Salary Cap Tracker**
Live cap figures, dead cap calculations, contract year visualization, cap space projections. The numbers are real. The decisions have real consequences within the sim.

**Free Agency**
Sign available free agents against your cap. Bidding logic, market value modeling, player interest simulation. The best players go to teams that manage their cap.

**Trades**
Propose and execute trades with AI-managed opposing GMs. Trade value modeling, draft pick compensation, fair deal detection. The AI does not make stupid trades.

**Draft (PFF Prospects)**
Full draft board powered by PFF prospect data. 7-round draft with positional needs weighting, trade-up/trade-down logic, and post-draft analysis.

**Season Simulation**
Full season sim with weekly results, playoff bracket, Super Bowl. Performance is influenced by roster quality, depth, and the decisions you made in the offseason.

**Polymarket Integration**
Live prediction market odds surfaced alongside sim results. See how real-money markets are pricing NFL outcomes and compare against your sim.

**PWA**
Progressive Web App — installable on any device, works offline, mobile-first. No app store required.

## The Audience

NFL fans who want more than fantasy football but are not in a position to play Madden franchise mode for 40 hours. The GM experience — the front office decisions, the cap chess, the draft strategy — is the most interesting part of the NFL for a large segment of fans who have never had a dedicated tool built for them.

This is also the DraftKings/FanDuel audience. They are already engaged, already spending money on NFL outcomes, already comfortable with a numbers-driven relationship with the NFL. They are the ideal target for both affiliate revenue and premium tier conversion.

## Monetization

**Google AdSense**
Display ads throughout the platform. Traffic-based, passive. Scales with user growth. Optimized ad placement around high-engagement moments (draft night, free agency signing, trade deadline).

**DraftKings / FanDuel Affiliate ($100-400 CPA)**
Integration of affiliate links, contextual placement during season simulation and Polymarket comparison views. When a user is deep in the sim and sees a matchup outcome, that is the moment to surface a DraftKings link. CPA rates of $100-400 per converted depositing user make this the highest-leverage revenue lever on the platform.

**Buy Me A Coffee**
Low-friction supporter channel for power users who want to contribute. No paywall, no friction — just an option for fans of the product.

**Premium Tier**
Advanced analytics, multi-season dynasty mode, expanded trade logic, exclusive draft content. Recurring subscription. Target: $9.99/month.
