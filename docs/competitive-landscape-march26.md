## PRODUCT LINE 1: Sharable AI Assistants (Jules)

### Competitors

**Poke (poke.com)**
- Raised $15M seed from General Catalyst. Launched September 2025.
- Lives in iMessage. Connects to email, calendar, files. Proactive — texts you before you ask.
- Pricing: users negotiate with a gatekeeper AI "bouncer." Starting price $75-$292/month, users haggle to $29+/month.
- Target: tech-savvy power users. The negotiation model disqualifies non-technical users.

**Linq (linqapp.com)**
- Raised $20M Series A (TQ Ventures, February 2026). Birmingham.
- API layer for businesses to send "blue bubble" iMessages instead of gray SMS.
- Not a consumer product. Infrastructure for brands.

**Meta AI** — Free across WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram. Not personal, not persistent, not configurable.

**WappGPT / Dume.ai** — WhatsApp-based AI assistants. $18-30/month. ChatGPT wrappers.

### 9's Differentiation
Jules is the only product designed to be:
1. Given to a non-technical person by someone else (gifted, not self-enrolled)
2. Zero-friction — works in existing iMessage, no app, no account, no negotiation
3. Personalized to a specific person
4. Private — data stays local

The gifting mechanic and non-technical target are genuinely unoccupied ground.

## PRODUCT LINE 2: AI Underwriting / Mortgage Guideline AI

### Competitors

**Guideline Buddy (guidelinebuddy.com)** ⚠️ DIRECT COMPETITOR
- AI chatbot for mortgage guidelines. Covers all 6 programs (Fannie, Freddie, FHA, VA, USDA, Non-QM).
- Cross-program comparison in one query. Auto-updates.
- Pricing: FREE tier (20 queries/month), Premium $12/month unlimited, Enterprise custom.

**Addy AI (addy.so)**
- Broader scope — document processing, loan summaries, guideline comparison, borrower follow-up.
- Enterprise pricing, likely $100-500/user/month.

**Tavant TOUCHLESS** — Powers 1 in 3 US mortgage loans. Multi-six-figure implementation. Top-50 lenders only.

**ICE Encompass AI** — SDK transition delayed to end of 2026. Slow pace. Not a guideline chatbot.

### 9's Edge vs. Guideline Buddy
- Voice-capable (Guideline Buddy is text-only)
- Lender overlay support (Rapid-specific overlays)
- Internal deployment (no data leaves Rapid's environment)
- Sweet spot pricing: $25-75/user/month

### Market Signal
55% of mortgage lenders plan to pilot AI in 2025. 30-50% operational cost reduction reported. 2.5x faster loan closings.

## PRODUCT LINE 3: AiNFLGM.com

### Competitors

**StickToTheModel (sticktothemodel.com)** ⚠️ CLOSEST COMPETITOR
- Free NFL analytics platform with GM Mode, Mock Draft Simulator.
- 500K+ draft simulations. Real OTC salary cap data.
- Completely free — no signup required.

**Pro Football Network — Ultimate GM Simulator** — Free web tool. Large established media site.

**PFF Mock Draft Simulator** — NFL official partnership. Draft-focused only.

### Market Size
- Global fantasy sports: $37.4B in 2026, growing 13-15% CAGR
- US alone: $10.4B revenue. 65M+ active players
- NFL = 74.9% of fantasy market

### 9's Edge vs. StickToTheModel
- AI-generated offseason narratives
- Season simulation integrated with offseason decisions
- Polymarket integration (unique)
- Mobile-first PWA, installable
- Summary export (PNG) for social sharing
- **Bottleneck is SEO/traffic, not product quality. Reddit posts need to go live.**

## CROSS-CUTTING FINDINGS

1. **Market is moving fast.** Poke raised $15M Sept 2025. Linq raised $20M Feb 2026. Guideline Buddy launched. Window is open but not indefinitely.
2. **Free is competitive reality.** Business models that work: enterprise or ad-supported + affiliate.
3. **No competitor is building what 9 does as a system.** They're individual products. 9's interconnected stack is defensible.
