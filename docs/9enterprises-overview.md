# 9 Enterprises LLC — Company Overview
**Founded:** 2026
**Jurisdiction:** Ohio LLC
**Mission:** Build AI-native businesses that prove what's possible when a founder partners with AI from day one.

---

## What We Are

9 Enterprises is an AI-first holding company. Every product we build tests a different dimension of one central thesis: **AI is not a feature. It is a business partner.**

We are not adding AI to existing software. We are building businesses where AI is the product — where the intelligence layer is load-bearing, not decorative.

**The Franchise** is the product portfolio. Three distinct products, three distinct markets, one shared infrastructure.

---

## The Proving Ground Model

Each product in The Franchise tests a specific hypothesis about AI-as-business-partner.

**Free Agents** tests: Can AI replace a personal assistant at consumer scale?
The bet is that most households could benefit from a dedicated AI assistant but would never set one up themselves. Zero-friction delivery through existing channels (iMessage, Telegram) removes the barrier entirely. Jules — the first Free Agent, deployed to Jasson's household — is the live proof of concept.

**AiNFLGM.com** tests: Can AI power a product that earns real affiliate and ad revenue at scale?
This is the consumer content play. 32-team NFL simulation platform with live roster data, cap tracking, trades, draft tools, and season sim. Already live. Monetized through DraftKings/FanDuel affiliate deals ($100-400 CPA), AdSense, and a premium tier. Target: $50K/month.

**AI Underwriting Team** tests: Can AI penetrate a B2B vertical where enterprise tools have priced out the mid-market?
Enterprise underwriting tools cost $200K+ and take six months to deploy. Mid-size mortgage lenders get nothing. A RAG-based guideline chatbot at $500-2K/month/lender is a 100x pricing advantage over the alternatives. Rapid Mortgage is the anchor customer.

---

## Why This Model Works

**Speed.** Building AI-native means no legacy to maintain, no technical debt from pre-AI architecture, no feature teams fighting over roadmap. The AI does the work. The founder ships.

**Leverage.** One small team with AI tooling can operate at the output level of a company 10x its size. We are not trying to compete with the big players on headcount. We compete on velocity and intelligence.

**Compounding infrastructure.** The voice server, Telegram integration, RAG pipeline, and cloud sync built for one product are immediately available to the next. Every product makes the next product cheaper to build.
