## Product 1: Free Agents
### *Sharable AI Agents & Assistants*

**Tagline:** Your people deserve their own AI. Give them one.

**What It Is:**
Personal AI assistants that work through the channels people already use — iMessage, SMS, Telegram. No apps to download. No accounts to create. No learning curve. Just text your AI and it handles things.

**First Iteration — Jules:**
Built for Jamie. A personal assistant that sends morning briefings, manages shopping lists, suggests meals, and handles reminders. All through iMessage. Zero friction.

Jules isn't a chatbot. Jules is a family member who never forgets, never sleeps, and never drops the ball.

**The Play:**
- **Phase 1:** Jules for the Fishback household (in progress)
- **Phase 2:** Template system — build a Jules for anyone in minutes
- **Phase 3:** Free Agents marketplace — sharable, customizable AI assistants anyone can deploy for their family, their team, their business
- **Revenue:** Subscription ($5-15/month per agent), premium templates, enterprise team packages

**Why It Matters:**
Every major AI company is building for developers and enterprises. Nobody is building AI assistants that a mom, a coach, or a small business owner can actually use. That's 300 million Americans who don't write code but would love a personal AI that works through their existing phone. Free Agents fills that gap.

## Product 3: AI Underwriting Team
### *Mortgage Guideline AI for Mid-Size Lenders*

**Tagline:** 900 pages of guidelines. One question. Five seconds.

**What It Is:**
A RAG-based AI chatbot that lets loan officers query mortgage agency guidelines in natural language and get accurate answers with exact source citations. No searching through PDFs. No calling underwriting. No guessing.

**The Problem It Solves:**
Loan officers at mid-size lenders like Rapid Mortgage spend hours every week searching through 900-page PDF guideline handbooks. The enterprise solutions (Tavant, ICE Encompass AI, LoanLogics) cost $200K+ and require dedicated IT departments. Mid-size lenders get nothing.

**Example:**
> **LO asks:** "What's the minimum credit score for FHA with 3.5% down?"
>
> **AI Underwriter:** Borrowers with a credit score of 580 or above are eligible for maximum financing (3.5% down payment). Borrowers between 500-579 are limited to 90% LTV. Below 500 is ineligible.
>
> *Source: HUD Handbook 4000.1, Section II.A.1.b*

Five seconds. Exact citation. Done.

**Coverage — All 5 Agencies:**

| Agency | Document | Pages |
|--------|----------|-------|
| FHA | HUD Handbook 4000.1 | ~900 |
| Fannie Mae | Selling Guide | ~1,200 |
| Freddie Mac | Seller/Servicer Guide | ~1,000 |
| VA | Pamphlet 26-7 | ~500 |
| USDA | HB-1-3555 | ~400 |

All publicly available. All free. All ingestible into the RAG pipeline.

**Roadmap:**
- **Phase 1 — FHA POC (2-3 days):** Ingest HUD 4000.1, build query interface, demo on a live call
- **Phase 2 — Agency Expansion (2-3 weeks):** All five agencies, single query interface
- **Phase 3 — Voice Integration:** LO asks question out loud mid-call, gets answer in 5 seconds hands-free

**Revenue Model:**
- SaaS subscription per lender ($500-2,000/month depending on LO count)
- Per-query pricing option for smaller shops
- Rapid Mortgage as the anchor customer and case study

**Why It Matters:**
The mortgage industry is a $2.3 trillion annual market. Mid-size lenders (100-500 employees) are the backbone of that market, and they have zero AI tools built for them. AI Underwriting Team doesn't compete with enterprise vendors — it serves the customers they ignore.
