# Google AdSense Research
## ainflgm.com Monetization Analysis

**Research Date:** March 25, 2026
**Prepared by:** UNO (Research Team Lead)
**Confidence Level:** High (requirements/process), Medium (revenue estimates — industry ranges)

---

## Summary

AdSense is viable for ainflgm.com but should not be the primary revenue strategy. Requirements are achievable — no official traffic minimum, content needs to be original and substantive. AI-generated content is acceptable if it provides genuine value. Revenue at modest traffic levels is low ($20-150/mo at 10k-50k monthly visitors) but AdSense can run passively alongside higher-value monetization like DraftKings/FanDuel affiliate links. Apply when the site has 15+ solid pages and consistent organic traffic.

---

## Requirements to Apply

### Traffic
- **No official minimum** — Google does not publish a traffic floor
- **Practical minimum:** 50-100 unique organic visitors per day (1,500-3,000/mo) before applying
- Low traffic won't get you rejected outright, but low traffic = near-zero earnings, which defeats the purpose
- Recommendation: wait until ainflgm.com has at least 3,000 monthly organic visitors before applying

### Content Requirements
- Minimum 15-25 substantive pages/posts
- Posts should be 800-1,500+ words that fully answer a user's question
- Must have: About page, Privacy Policy page, Contact page
- Content must be original — not scraped or copied from other sources
- Site must be at least a few weeks old (ideally 3+ months) with consistent publishing

### Technical Requirements
- Site must be accessible, fast-loading, and mobile-friendly
- Must have an XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
- No broken pages, no under-construction sections
- Custom domain required (no free subdomains)

### Prohibited Content
- Gambling content in certain forms — **important for ainflgm.com**
  - Sports analysis and fantasy sports content is generally fine
  - Direct gambling facilitation or promotion of unregulated gambling sites is not
  - DraftKings/FanDuel are licensed operators — linking to them as affiliates is not a disqualifier
  - However, heavy gambling promotion could trigger policy review — keep AdSense placements on analysis/stats content, not directly adjacent to gambling CTAs

## AI Content Policy

**Current stance (2026):** Google accepts AI-generated content if it meets their E-E-A-T standard (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).

- Raw, unedited AI output is risky — Google's detection has improved significantly
- AI content that provides genuine analysis, unique data, or real value is acceptable
- The key test: "Does this content help the user, or is it just filler?"

**For ainflgm.com specifically:**
- AI-generated NFL analysis that draws on real game data and provides actionable picks = acceptable
- Generic AI summaries that add no unique value = risky
- Recommendation: ensure each page reflects actual data analysis, not just regurgitated stats

## Revenue Estimates

### Sports Niche RPM (Revenue Per 1,000 Pageviews)

Sports content is a mid-tier niche for AdSense — not as lucrative as finance or legal, but solid.

| Traffic Level | Monthly Pageviews | Estimated RPM | Est. Monthly Revenue |
|--------------|------------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Early stage | 5,000 | $2-4 | $10-20 |
| Getting traction | 20,000 | $3-6 | $60-120 |
| Solid growth | 50,000 | $4-8 | $200-400 |
| Real traffic | 100,000 | $5-10 | $500-1,000 |
| Scale | 500,000 | $6-12 | $3,000-6,000 |

**Key variables:**
- US-based traffic earns $8-15 RPM vs $0.50-2.50 for international
- NFL content (high advertiser demand) will be toward the top of the sports range
- Traffic from search (organic) earns more than social traffic
- Fantasy sports season (Aug-Jan) will outperform offseason

**Honest Assessment:** At current traffic levels, AdSense revenue will be minimal — probably $20-50/month early on. The real value comes after scale. Do not apply AdSense as a primary monetization strategy at launch.

## Application Process — Step by Step

1. **Prepare the site**
   - Add Privacy Policy page (required — use a generator like termsfeed.com)
   - Add About page and Contact page
   - Ensure Google Search Console is set up and sitemap submitted
   - Verify at least 15 substantive, unique pages exist

2. **Apply at adsense.google.com**
   - Sign in with a Google account
   - Enter your website URL
   - Select your country
   - Accept terms of service
   - Click "Start using AdSense"

3. **Add the AdSense code to your site**
   - Google gives you a small JavaScript snippet
   - Add it to the `<head>` section of your site's HTML (or use the React/JSX equivalent)
   - This allows Google to crawl and evaluate your site

4. **Wait for review**
   - Google's crawlers evaluate the site
   - You will get an email: approved, rejected, or needs changes
   - If approved, ads start showing automatically

5. **Verify identity and get paid**
   - Once you earn $10, Google mails a PIN to your address
   - Enter the PIN in your AdSense dashboard to verify
   - Payments are monthly, via direct deposit, when balance reaches $100

## Approval Timeline

- **Initial review:** 2-4 weeks (sometimes faster, sometimes slower)
- **Final approval:** Up to 2 additional weeks after initial review
- **Total:** Plan for 2-6 weeks from application to live ads
- Rejected applications can be resubmitted after fixing identified issues

## Recommendation

**Apply AdSense but do not prioritize it.** The DraftKings/FanDuel affiliate programs (researched previously) will generate far more revenue per click than AdSense at low-to-medium traffic levels. Consider this order of operations:

1. **Now:** Set up affiliate links (DraftKings, FanDuel) — higher CPAs, no approval wait
2. **At 3,000+ monthly visitors:** Apply for AdSense as passive background income
3. **At 50,000+ monthly visitors:** AdSense becomes meaningful ($200-400/mo) — worth optimizing ad placement

AdSense and affiliate links can run simultaneously — they do not conflict.
