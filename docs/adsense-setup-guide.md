# Google AdSense Setup Guide for ainflgm.com

## Steps to Apply

1. **Go to:** https://adsense.google.com/start/
2. **Sign in** with a Google account (recommend creating one specifically for AiNFL GM)
3. **Enter site URL:** ainflgm.com
4. **Country:** United States
5. **Accept Terms**

## What Google Reviews
- Site must have original content (YES — our simulator is 100% original)
- Site must have a privacy policy page (NEED TO ADD)
- Site should have About and Contact pages (NEED TO ADD)
- Site must be navigable and user-friendly (YES)
- Site should have at least 30 pages of content (our 32 team pages + 8 feature pages = 40+)
- No prohibited content (YES — sports content is approved)

## What We Need Before Applying
1. **Privacy Policy page** — Required. Must disclose use of cookies, analytics, and ad serving.
2. **About page** — Recommended. Builds trust with AdSense reviewers.
3. **Contact page** — Recommended. Can be a simple email form.
4. **Payment info** — Jasson needs to add bank account for payouts.

## Ad Placement Strategy
- **Footer leaderboard** (728x90) — Already coded, just needs AdSense code
- **Between content sections** — Can add on longer pages (Summary, Draft)
- **Sidebar** on desktop — Could add for wider screens
- **Native ads** — Blended into content feed (markets, roster lists)

## Expected Revenue
At current traffic levels (estimated 100-500 visits/day during draft season):
- Sports content CPM: $5-15
- Conservative estimate: $5-25/day = $150-750/month during peak season
- Off-season: $1-5/day = $30-150/month

## IMPORTANT: What NOT to Do
- Never click your own ads
- Never ask others to click ads
- Never place ads where they might be accidentally clicked
- Don't place more than 3 ad units per page initially
