> Most NFL fans think about free agency wrong. A quick thread on cap math that will change how you watch the offseason. [1/5]

> The salary cap is not just about what you spend NOW. It's about what you owe LATER. A $30M/yr deal that backloads to $40M in year 3 is a trap. [2/5]

> The best teams think 2-3 years ahead. They extend stars before the market inflates. They avoid the top of the free agent market unless they absolutely have to be there. [4/5]
