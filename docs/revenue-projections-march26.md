- Ohio sports betting market fully mature (legalized Jan 2023). Cincinnati user base is already saturated by major platforms — national traffic matters more than local.

### Prediction Markets
- **Polymarket** referral program still active. Referral bonuses vary ($5-$25 per funded account).
- **Kalshi** affiliate program more structured now — $10-$20 per funded account.
- Prediction markets seeing regulatory tailwinds after 2024 election cycle success. Growing mainstream awareness helps conversion rates.

### Premium / Subscription
- Competitive landscape: PFF Pro at $9.99/mo, FantasyPros premium at $7.99/mo. Our $4.99/mo price point remains competitive for a niche tool.
- Stripe integration cost: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction.
