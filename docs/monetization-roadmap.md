| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |

| Affiliate rejection (all programs) | 20% | Medium | Apply to 5+ programs; at least 1-2 will approve |
| Low traffic growth | 25% | High | Double down on Reddit, add blog content, explore paid ads |
| NFL lockout/strike | 5% | Very High | Diversify to other sports/content |
| Google algorithm change | 15% | Medium | Diversify traffic sources (email, social, direct) |
| Stripe integration issues | 10% | Medium | Use tested Stripe Checkout; minimal custom code |
