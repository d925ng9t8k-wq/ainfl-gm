About AiNFL GM

Built by 9 Enterprises • Cincinnati, OH • 2026

AiNFL GM is a free, AI-powered NFL offseason simulator built for fans who want to go deeper than a box score. Pick any of the 32 NFL teams, take over the front office, manage the salary cap, sign free agents, make trades, run the draft, and simulate a full NFL season — all with real 2026 data and no signup required.

This is not a basic "build your team" widget. AiNFL GM models the complexity of the real NFL: dead cap, restructured contracts, compensatory picks, positional draft value, and AI-managed trade partners that actually push back on bad deals. The goal is to give every fan the tools that NFL front offices use, at zero cost.

It was built by 9 Enterprises — an AI-first product studio that handles engineering, research, and operations. Not a chatbot. Not an assistant. A co-builder that ships production code.

The entire product — code, design, data pipeline, deployment — was built with AI as the primary execution layer. AiNFL GM is proof that a single founder with the right AI partner can build and ship a real product, fast.

What You Can Do

AiNFL GM covers the full NFL offseason cycle, from the end of one season to the kickoff of the next. Here is what the platform gives you:

  • Salary Cap Management — Work with the real 2026 NFL cap of $301.2 million. Cut players, restructure contracts, and see dead cap implications in real time before you commit.
  • Free Agency — Browse the available free agent pool, make contract offers, and balance short-term wins against long-term cap flexibility the same way real GMs do.
  • Trade Analyzer — Propose multi-player, multi-pick trades against AI-controlled teams. The AI evaluates deals based on team needs and market value — it will not accept lopsided offers.
  • 7-Round Mock Draft — Simulate a full 7-round draft against AI opponents picking based on PFF grades, team needs, and real draft board logic.
  • Season Simulation — After building your roster, simulate a full NFL season and see your projected win total, playoff odds, and division finish.
  • Prediction Markets — Polymarket odds are displayed alongside sim results so you can see how real-money markets price the outcomes you are modeling.

The Tools Suite

Beyond the main GM simulator, AiNFL GM includes over 50 free NFL tools and games accessible at ainflgm.com/tools. These cover everything from cap health grades and draft trade calculators to trivia games, wordle-style challenges, and NFL stat leaders. Every tool is mobile-friendly and requires no account or download.

Some of the most popular tools include:

  • Cap Space Rankings — All 32 teams ranked by 2026 cap space with cap health grades and top contract breakdowns
  • Draft Trade Calculator — The Jimmy Johnson trade value chart, fully interactive, for evaluating any pick swap
  • Team Needs Analyzer — Position-by-position need ratings for all 32 teams heading into the draft
  • Season Simulator — Run a full 2026 NFL season sim and generate projected standings, playoff seedings, and Super Bowl odds
  • Award Predictor — AI-powered predictions for MVP, DPOY, Offensive Rookie of the Year, and all major NFL awards
  • NFL Wordle — A daily word game using NFL player names, playable once a day
  • Cap Impact Calculator — Model the cap hit and dead money for any hypothetical extension or restructure
  • Bengals Cap Tracker — Deep dive into the Cincinnati Bengals' 2026 cap situation, player by player

Our Mission

Most sports analysis tools are either too simple (basic stats dashboards) or too expensive (professional scouting software). AiNFL GM exists in the middle: sophisticated enough to model real NFL cap decisions and draft strategy, simple enough that any fan can use it in five minutes with no tutorial.

The mission is to democratize the kind of analysis that only front offices and professional scouts have had access to — real cap math, real positional value models, real AI trade partners — and make it free for every fan. We think better-informed fans make the game more interesting for everyone.
