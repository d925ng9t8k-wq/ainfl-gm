About AiNFL GM

Built by 9 Enterprises • Cincinnati, OH • 2026

AiNFL GM is a free, AI-powered NFL offseason simulator. Pick any of the 32 NFL teams, manage the salary cap, sign free agents, make trades, run the draft, and simulate the season.

It was built by Jasson Fishback and 9 — an AI partner that handles engineering, research, and operations. Not a chatbot. Not an assistant. A co-builder.

The entire product — code, design, data pipeline, deployment — was built with AI as the primary execution layer. AiNFL GM is proof that a single founder with the right AI partner can build and ship a real product.

What Makes It Different

Real Cap Math

Salary cap data from OverTheCap. Restructures, dead money, cap hits — the numbers work like the real NFL.

AI Opponents

Trade with AI-managed GMs that won't accept lopsided deals. Draft against AI opponents that pick based on PFF rankings and positional needs.

Full Offseason → Season Sim

Your GM decisions directly influence season outcomes. Build a roster, then see if it wins. Most tools stop at the draft — we simulate the whole thing.

Prediction Markets

Polymarket odds displayed alongside your sim results. See how real money markets price the outcomes you're simulating.
