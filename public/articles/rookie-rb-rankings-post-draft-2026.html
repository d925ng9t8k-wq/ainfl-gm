Running Back Opportunity Map

The single most important variable for rookie running back dynasty value is opportunity — specifically, how open the backfield is at their landing team. The 2026 class is top-heavy: Jeanty is in a class by himself. The gap between him and the second-tier backs is significant.

The critical windows to watch in Rounds 2-3: teams like the Pittsburgh Steelers, Denver Broncos, Minnesota Vikings, and Green Bay Packers all have backfield vacancy. Any of the Tier 2 backs landing in those spots vaults into the 1.02-1.04 rookie draft range.

The teams to avoid as landing spots: Kansas City Chiefs (Pacheco entrenched), Buffalo Bills (James Cook entrenched), Philadelphia Eagles (Saquon Barkley entrenched). Any rookie landing behind a locked-in feature back is a stash-only in dynasty regardless of talent.
