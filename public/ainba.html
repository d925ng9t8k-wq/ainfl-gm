2025-26 NBA Salary Cap

$154.6M

The league-wide cap for the 2025-26 season. Go over and you're in luxury tax territory. Go past the aprons and your options shrink fast.

  • $154.6M — Salary cap
  • $187.9M — Luxury tax threshold
  • $195.9M — First apron
  • $207.8M — Second apron (hard cap)
