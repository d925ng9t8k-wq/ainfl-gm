FAQ

QUESTIONS? ANSWERS.

Do I need to install an app?+
No. FreeAgent9 works through regular text messages (SMS/iMessage). You also get access to a web chat dashboard, but the core experience is texting a phone number.
How accurate are the guideline answers?+
Our guideline knowledge base is built from official sources: FHA Handbook, Fannie Mae Selling Guide, Freddie Mac guides, VA Lender's Handbook, and USDA Rural Development guidelines. Answers include citations so you can verify. That said, always confirm with your underwriter on edge cases.
Is my data secure?+
We never store borrower PII (Social Security numbers, account numbers, etc.). Conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell or share your data. Your agent's memory is isolated — no other user can access it.
Can I use this for my whole branch/team?+
Yes. Each team member gets their own dedicated number and personalized agent. Volume pricing is available for teams of 5+. Contact us for details.
What if I work in real estate or insurance, not mortgage?+
FreeAgent9 is built for any sales-driven professional who lives on their phone. We customize the knowledge base, morning briefing, and features for your industry. Mortgage is our first vertical, with real estate and insurance coming next.
How long is the free trial?+
14 days, full access, no credit card required. If you do not love it, just stop texting. No cancellation hoops to jump through.
Where does the rate data come from?+
Rate data is sourced from public market feeds and MBS tracking services. For Starter users, you can also text your agent your own rate sheet data for the most precise alerts. Pro users get integrated real-time rate feeds.
