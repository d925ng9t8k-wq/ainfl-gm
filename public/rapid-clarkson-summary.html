3 The P&L Branch Model

This is not a standard net branch. It is a true P&L partnership where Austin and his team control the economics of their branch from day one.

The core idea: You run your branch like your own company. You control the margins, the pricing, the hiring, and the culture. We provide the license, the warehouse lines, the compliance infrastructure, and the back-office support. Your net profit is your profit.

How It Works

  • Revenue: Your branch books all revenue from closed loans at the margin you set.
  • Expenses: Branch operating costs (processors, marketing, office) come off the top.
  • Net Profit: What remains after expenses is split with a heavily favorable margin share in your direction.
  • Full Transparency: Monthly P&L statements. You see every dollar in and every dollar out.
