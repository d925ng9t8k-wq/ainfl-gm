Product 1

Free Agents

Your people deserve their own AI. Give them one.

What It Is

Personal AI assistants that work through the channels people already use — iMessage, SMS, Telegram. No apps to download. No accounts to create. No learning curve. Just text your AI and it handles things.

Jules — First Iteration

Built for Jamie. A personal assistant that sends morning briefings, manages shopping lists, suggests meals, and handles reminders. All through iMessage. Zero friction.

Jules isn't a chatbot. Jules is a family member who never forgets, never sleeps, and never drops the ball.

Jamie texts: "What should I make for dinner? Jude doesn't want pasta again."
Jules: "How about chicken tacos? You've got everything — chicken breasts, tortillas, the taco seasoning in the back of the pantry. Takes about 25 minutes. Want me to add anything to the shopping list while we're at it?"

Product 3

AI Underwriting Team

900 pages of guidelines. One question. Five seconds.

What It Is

A RAG-based AI chatbot that lets mortgage loan officers query agency guidelines in plain English and get accurate answers with exact source citations. No PDF searching. No calling underwriting. No guessing.

POC Ready to Build

Loan Officer asks: "Can I use rental income from a property the borrower just bought to qualify for an FHA loan?"
AI Underwriter: Rental income from a recently acquired property is generally not eligible for FHA qualification unless the borrower has a two-year history of managing rental properties documented on their tax returns. Exceptions apply for relocations where the departing residence becomes a rental.
Source: HUD Handbook 4000.1, Section II.A.4.d — Rental Income

That answer takes a loan officer 15-20 minutes to find manually. The AI does it in five seconds. Multiply by 10 questions/day, 20 LOs, 250 working days. That's the ROI conversation.

The Competitive Gap

Enterprise tools (Tavant, ICE Encompass AI, LoanLogics) start at $200,000+ implementation with 6-month deployments and dedicated IT requirements. Built for the top 50 lenders by volume.

Mid-size lenders get nothing. Their LOs are still Ctrl+F-ing through 900-page PDFs. That's a daily time drain, a compliance risk, and a training burden that never goes away.

Competitive Landscape

Research by UNO • March 26, 2026

Free Agents vs. Market Gap Confirmed

Poke raised $15M (General Catalyst) for iMessage AI — but charges $29-292/mo and targets tech-savvy users. Their signup makes you negotiate price with an AI bouncer. Linq raised $20M but is B2B API infrastructure, not consumer.

  • Nobody does "gift an AI to a non-technical person" — that's unclaimed ground
  • Zero-friction channel delivery (no app, no account, no negotiation)
  • Privacy — all data stays local, no third-party platform

AI Underwriter vs. Guideline Buddy Direct Competitor

Guideline Buddy exists at $12/month unlimited. Covers all 6 programs. Auto-updates. This validates the market but sets a price floor.

  • Voice-capable — Guideline Buddy is text-only. LOs need hands-free mid-call.
  • Lender overlay support — Rapid-specific overlays baked in
  • Internal deployment — no data leaves Rapid's environment
  • Sweet spot: $25-75/user/mo (premium over $12, 100x cheaper than $200K enterprise)

AiNFLGM vs. StickToTheModel Indirect

StickToTheModel is free with 500K+ draft sims. Closest head-to-head. Market is $37.4B globally, NFL = 74.9% of fantasy share.

  • AI-generated offseason narratives — not just mechanics
  • Season sim tied to GM decisions — they don't have this
  • Polymarket integration — unique angle
  • Mobile-first PWA — they're desktop-optimized
  • Bottleneck is traffic/SEO, not product quality. Reddit posts need to go live.

Cross-cutting finding: No competitor is building what 9 does as a system. They're all individual products. The interconnected stack across consumer, entertainment, and enterprise — sharing voice infra, comms layer, and AI model — is the moat.
