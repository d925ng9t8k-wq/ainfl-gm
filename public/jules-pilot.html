01
What Is Pilot?

Pilot is a personal AI assistant that runs entirely over text message. No app to download. No account to create. No login. You get a phone number, you save it as Pilot, and you just text it.

Pilot is built specifically for your life as a loan officer. It knows mortgage guidelines, tracks your reminders, writes your social posts, and sends you a morning briefing before you open Encompass. Think of it as having a sharp assistant in your phone who already knows what you do for a living.

It responds in seconds. Works at 6 AM or 11 PM. Remembers everything you've told it.

How It Works
  1. You get a dedicated phone number. We'll text you from it to introduce Pilot. Save it as "Pilot" in your contacts.
  2. Text like you would anyone else. Ask a question, set a reminder, ask for today's rates. Plain English. No commands, no syntax.
  3. Pilot responds in seconds. Short, direct answers. No corporate speak. It knows you're a producing branch manager who's in between calls.
  4. Pilot remembers your context. Your team names, your preferences, your open reminders — Pilot carries it across every conversation.
  5. 7:00 AM — morning briefing arrives. Before you've opened anything, Pilot has already done the morning homework. You just read it and move.

07
What Pilot Is NOT
  • Not a replacement for Encompass or your CRM. Pilot is a layer on top of your existing tools, not a substitute for them.
  • Not handling sensitive borrower data. No SSNs, no loan files, no financials. Pilot knows names and reminder context — that's it.
  • Not making decisions for you. Pilot surfaces information and answers faster. The judgment calls are still yours.
  • Not an AI that hallucinates guidelines. Mortgage guideline lookups are pulled from a verified reference table — not made up. If Pilot isn't sure, it says so.
  • Not something that will spam you. Pilot texts you once in the morning and then waits for you to reach out. No check-ins, no "how's your day going."
