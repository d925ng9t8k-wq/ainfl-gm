01
What Is Jules?

Jules is a personal AI assistant that runs entirely over text message. No app to download. No account to create. No login. You get a phone number, you save it as Jules, and you just text it.

Jules is built specifically for your life as a loan officer. It knows mortgage guidelines, tracks your reminders, and sends you a morning briefing before you open Encompass. Think of it as having a sharp assistant in your phone who already knows what you do for a living.

It responds in seconds. Works at 6 AM or 11 PM. Remembers everything you've told it.

03
How It Works
  1. You get a dedicated phone number. We'll text you from it to introduce Jules. Save it as "Jules" in your contacts.
  2. Text like you would anyone else. Ask a question, set a reminder, ask for today's rates. Plain English. No commands, no syntax.
  3. Jules responds in seconds. Short, direct answers. No corporate speak. It knows you're a producing branch manager who's in between calls.
  4. Jules remembers your context. Your team names, your preferences, your open reminders — Jules carries it across every conversation.
  5. 7:00 AM — morning briefing arrives. Before you've opened anything, Jules has already done the morning homework. You just read it and move.

05
What Jules Is NOT
  • Not a replacement for Encompass or your CRM. Jules is a layer on top of your existing tools, not a substitute for them.
  • Not handling sensitive borrower data. No SSNs, no loan files, no financials. Jules knows names and reminder context — that's it.
  • Not making decisions for you. Jules surfaces information and answers faster. The judgment calls are still yours.
  • Not an AI that hallucinates guidelines. Mortgage guideline lookups are pulled from a verified reference table — not made up. If Jules isn't sure, it says so.
  • Not something that will spam you. Jules texts you once in the morning and then waits for you to reach out. No check-ins, no "how's your day going."
