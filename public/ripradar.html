📖 Know Before You Rip
🏪
Blaster Box
Retail boxes you find at Target or Walmart. Usually 6–8 packs. Best starting point for beginners. Lower price = lower odds of big hits, but still fun to open.
🏝
Hobby Box
From card shops or online. More packs, better odds, guaranteed autographs in many sets. These are what you see in break videos on YouTube.
🏆
Hobby Jumbo
The big leagues. More cards per pack, better hit odds, guaranteed high-end content. Expensive — usually $500-$1,200+ — but best chance at silver prizms and numbered cards.
🔥
Chase Cards
The rare inserts everyone is hunting for. Short prints, numbered parallels, 1-of-1s, autographs. Kaboom!, Downtown, Silver Prizm — these are what make a box worth ripping.
🎲
Hype Level
RipRadar's score (1–10) for how hot a set is right now. Based on rookie quality, resale prices, and collector demand. Higher = more people chasing this set.
👑
Rookie Cards (RC)
A player's first official card from their rookie NFL season. These are the most valuable cards for new players. Always look for the RC tag on the card.
