## Standard Bio (150 words)

Jasson Fishback is the founder and CEO of 9 Enterprises LLC, an AI-powered holding company based in Cincinnati, Ohio. Fishback co-owns Rapid Mortgage Company, a leading Ohio independent mortgage bank specializing in FHA, VA, USDA, and conventional lending.

In 2026, Fishback founded 9enterprises to test a singular hypothesis: that artificial intelligence has crossed the threshold where a single founder, partnered with an autonomous AI, can build and operate a multi-company portfolio without hiring employees. The company's portfolio spans consumer products, B2B SaaS, financial technology, and education.

Fishback works closely with 9 — a fully autonomous AI partner — who handles day-to-day operations, product development, and strategic execution across all 11 portfolio companies. The result is a new model for solo entrepreneurship: one human with vision, one AI with execution capability, operating at enterprise scale.
