# 9 Enterprises Brand Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0 — April 2026

---

## Brand Name Usage

| Context | Correct Form |
|---------|-------------|
| Legal documents | 9 Enterprises LLC |
| Product brand, URLs, social | 9enterprises |
| Referring to the AI partner | 9 |
| Headline copy | 9enterprises |

**Rules:**
- Never "9 Enterprises" (with space) in product or marketing context
- Never "Nine Enterprises"
- Product names are always lowercase + the number 9: `freeagent9`, `trader9`, `agent9`, `x9`
- Exception: proper names like "PlayAiGM" retain their casing

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Background | `#0a0a0a` | Primary background |
| Surface | `#111111` | Cards, panels |
| Border | `#222222` | Dividers, card borders |
| Text Primary | `#f0f0f0` | Headlines, primary copy |
| Text Secondary | `#888888` | Body copy, descriptions |
| Accent / Brand Orange | `#f97316` | CTAs, highlights, the "9" mark |
| Accent Bright | `#FB4F14` | Gradient terminus, hover states |

**The orange (`#f97316`) is the signature brand color.** It represents the Bengals connection, the energy of the founder's mission, and the heat of autonomous operation. It is used sparingly — never as a fill on large surfaces, always as a signal.

---

## Typography

- **Primary:** -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif
- **Headlines:** 800 weight, -0.03em to -0.04em letter-spacing, tight line-height (1.0–1.15)
- **Body:** 400–500 weight, 1.6–1.85 line-height
- **Labels/eyebrows:** 600 weight, 0.08–0.1em letter-spacing, uppercase, 0.7–0.8rem

## Logo

The primary logo mark is the numeral "9" in the brand orange (`#f97316`) on the dark background (`#0a0a0a`). Font weight: 800. The wordmark is "9enterprises" — the "9" in orange, "enterprises" in white.

**Logo files:**
- `favicon.svg` — SVG favicon (64x64 equivalent)
- `9-brand.jpg` — Apple touch icon / social avatar
- `9e-og-preview.jpg` — Open Graph image (1200x630)

**Clearspace:** Maintain at minimum the width of the "9" character as clearspace on all sides.

**Do not:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Use on white or light backgrounds without inverting colors
- Change the orange to any other color
- Add effects (drop shadows, gradients applied to the mark itself)

## Photography & Imagery

- Dark backgrounds only. No light, airy, stock-photo aesthetics.
- AI imagery: abstract, geometric, circuit-adjacent — but never cliche robot imagery
- Human photography: authentic, not posed. Cincinnati / Midwest sensibility.
- Video: produced via HeyGen Pro with 9enterprises-approved avatars

---

## Voice & Tone

**What we are:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Technical, not jargony
- Ambitious, not hype-driven
- Direct, not blunt
- Proof-driven, not claim-heavy

**What we are not:**
- Corporate. Never say "synergy," "leverage," "paradigm shift"
- Hype. Never claim things we haven't proven
- Apologetic. We're building something real and we know it

**The single most important sentence for brand voice:**
"One founder. One AI. Zero employees. Real businesses."

## Usage Permissions

For press and media:
- Logo and brand assets may be used in editorial coverage of 9 Enterprises LLC
- All product names and company name must be used in their correct form (see Brand Name Usage above)
- Do not use imagery or copy to imply endorsement without written consent from 9 Enterprises LLC
