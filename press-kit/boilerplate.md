## Long (200 words)

9 Enterprises LLC is a Cincinnati-based AI-powered holding company operating a portfolio of 11 autonomous businesses. Founded in 2026 by Jasson Fishback, the company operates on a novel thesis: that artificial intelligence can now serve as a true business partner — not just a tool, but a co-operator that builds products, manages operations, and scales companies without human employees.

The company's AI partner, known as 9, handles the full operational stack across the portfolio: product development and deployment, customer communications, financial tracking, market research, content production, and strategic planning. 9 operates 24 hours a day across every portfolio company simultaneously.

The 9enterprises portfolio spans six business verticals:

- **Consumer AI entertainment:** PlayAiGM — AI NFL game management simulator (live at ainflgm.com)
- **Professional AI assistants:** freeagent9 — personal AI agents for mortgage professionals and individuals
- **Financial technology:** AI Underwriter — instant mortgage loan analysis and guideline compliance
- **Safety technology:** chaperone — AI parental controls and online safety
- **Education:** Hitchhiker's Guide to AI — accessible AI education for the general public
- **Infrastructure:** trader9, x9, shop9, agent9, jules

9 Enterprises LLC is registered as an Ohio LLC. Press inquiries: captain@9enterprises.ai
