## What is AINFL GM?

AINFL GM lets you be the General Manager of any NFL team. Make real offseason decisions using actual 2026 NFL data:

- **📋 Roster Management** — View your full roster with real salary cap data from Over The Cap. Restructure contracts, cut players, or extend deals with realistic NFL contract mechanics.

- **💰 Salary Cap Tracker** — $301.2M cap with real dead money, cap savings, and position-group breakdowns. Smart cut suggestions based on actual contract terms.

- **✍️ Free Agency** — Sign from 90+ real unsigned free agents. Full contract negotiation with AAV, signing bonus, guaranteed money, and year-1 cap hit calculations. Players accept or decline based on market value.

- **🔄 Trade Simulator** — Trade players and draft picks (2026, 2027, 2028) with any team. Real roster data for all 32 teams. Cap impact analysis, dead money calculations, and Jimmy Johnson trade value chart.

- **🎯 Mock Draft** — Full 1-7 round draft simulator with 224+ real 2026 prospects from PFF. CPU teams draft with position-need awareness and realistic variance. Trade up, trade down, or propose custom deals mid-draft.

- **📊 Offseason Summary** — Grades for every phase (FA, trades, draft). Pre-season baseline from real 2026 moves. Exportable results.

## Data Sources

- Salary cap data: [Over The Cap](https://overthecap.com)
- Draft prospects: [PFF](https://pff.com), [Tankathon](https://tankathon.com)
- Free agents: [Spotrac](https://spotrac.com)
- Transactions: ESPN, NFL.com, CBS Sports
