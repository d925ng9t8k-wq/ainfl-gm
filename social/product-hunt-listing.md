# AiNFL GM - Product Hunt Listing

## Tagline (60 chars max)
AI-powered NFL GM simulator: cap, trades, draft, season

## Description
AiNFL GM lets you step into the shoes of an NFL general manager for any of the 32 NFL teams. Built with real salary cap data, actual rosters, and AI-driven player evaluations, this free browser-based simulator lets you manage the entire offseason -- from cutting players and signing free agents to executing trades and running the NFL draft. Then simulate a full 17-game season to see how your moves pan out.

No login required. No downloads. Just pick your team and start building a championship roster.

## Key Features
- **All 32 NFL Teams** -- Real rosters with accurate salary cap data from Over The Cap
- **Salary Cap Management** -- Cut, restructure, and extend contracts with real dead money calculations
- **Free Agency** -- Sign free agents with AI-generated market valuations and contract offers
- **Trade Engine** -- Propose and execute trades with AI-powered trade value analysis
- **NFL Draft Simulator** -- Full 7-round mock draft with AI draft board and pick trading
- **Season Simulation** -- Simulate a 17-game NFL season with AI-powered game outcomes
- **Offseason Summary** -- Review every move you made and share your results
- **Mobile Friendly** -- Fully responsive PWA that works on any device
- **100% Free** -- No accounts, no paywalls, no ads

## Screenshots Descriptions
1. **Team Roster View** -- The main roster page showing all players, their positions, cap hits, and contract details for the selected team. Clean dark UI with team-colored accents.
2. **Salary Cap Tracker** -- Visual breakdown of cap space, dead money, and savings from roster moves. Shows running total as you cut and restructure players.
3. **Free Agency Market** -- Browse available free agents sorted by position and overall rating. AI-generated market values help you make competitive offers.
4. **Trade Interface** -- Build trade packages between teams. AI evaluates trade fairness and provides instant feedback on proposed deals.
5. **Draft Board** -- Full draft simulator with big board rankings, team needs, and pick-by-pick AI selections. Trade up or down to target your guy.
6. **Season Simulation** -- Week-by-week season results with standings, playoff picture, and game summaries.

## Maker Comment
Hey everyone! I built AiNFL GM because I wanted a realistic NFL offseason simulator that didn't require downloading anything or creating an account. As a lifelong Bengals fan, I started building this for Cincinnati but quickly expanded to all 32 teams.

Every roster and salary cap number is pulled from real sources (Over The Cap, ESPN). The AI evaluations use a custom model trained on historical NFL data to generate realistic free agent markets, trade values, and draft outcomes.

The whole thing runs in your browser -- no backend needed. Pick any team, manage the offseason, then simulate a full season to see if your moves lead to a Super Bowl.
